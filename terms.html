7. Death Verification, Delivery Triggers & Lifetime Definition

"Lifetime access" means the period from your enrollment date through the earlier of: (a) your natural death, or (b) your 99th birthday. Subscribers who reach age 99 without triggering delivery will be contacted by Afterwords to renew, update their vault, or designate a successor vault administrator. Afterwords reserves the right to archive inactive vaults after age 99 with 90 days' prior written notice.

Delivery is triggered by the following three-tier protocol, applied in order:

  • Tier 1 — Trusted contact confirmation. Your designated trusted contact affirmatively notifies Afterwords of your death and requests delivery. This is the fastest and preferred trigger.
  • Tier 2 — Published legal notice. Afterwords reserves the right, but is not obligated, to monitor North Carolina newspapers of record and official legal notice publications — including county-level probate notices published pursuant to N.C. Gen. Stat. § 28A-14-1 — for notices indicating the death of a subscriber. A published Notice to Creditors, Notice of Estate Administration, or equivalent official legal notice naming a subscriber constitutes sufficient evidence of death to initiate delivery under this agreement, without further confirmation from the trusted contact. This tier applies only where probate proceedings are initiated; many estates are never probated, and Afterwords makes no representation that it will discover any given notice.
  • Tier 3 — Automated check-in trigger (final backstop). Six (6) consecutive missed monthly check-ins, following two (2) missed check-ins that each generated an unanswered emergency contact attempt, constitute conclusive and sufficient evidence for Afterwords to initiate vault delivery. This trigger is unconditional. No probate proceeding, no trusted contact action, and no public announcement of any kind is required. Afterwords' obligation to deliver is fully satisfied by compliance with this three-tier protocol, regardless of the circumstances of the subscriber's death or the absence of any estate proceeding.

Afterwords does not monitor obituaries, social media feeds, government death registries, or commercial death-notification services. The three-tier protocol above is the exclusive basis for delivery.

False triggers. If delivery is triggered in error — due to subscriber incapacity, hospitalization, travel, or technical failure — Afterwords' liability is limited to reasonable efforts to halt undelivered messages. Messages already sent cannot be recalled. This limitation is a material term of this agreement and a condition of the introductory pricing offered.
